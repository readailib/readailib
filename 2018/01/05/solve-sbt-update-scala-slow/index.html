解决sbt更新scala速度慢

Heng-Heng Shen  •  2018/01/05  •  1 min read

在国内如果要使用sbt更新scala时，速度是一个硬伤，因为国内访问maven库的速度确实很慢，因此在这里给出相应的解决方案。

编辑sbtconfig.txt文件

打开sbt的安装位置，比如在我的机器(\(\mathtt{Windows}\))上就是\(\mathtt{E:\backslash Program Files (x86) \backslash sbt}\)文件夹，打开文件夹下面的\(\mathtt{conf\backslash sbtconfig.txt}\)文件，增加一行

-Dsbt.override.build.repos=true

编辑用户文件夹下repositories文件

打开本地用户目录，比如我的机器上上的目录为\(\mathtt{C:\backslash Users\backslash Shine}\)目录，找到\(\mathtt{.sbt}\)目录，在该目录下创建一个新的文件\(\mathtt{repositories}\)文件，编辑内容为

[repositories]
local
aliyun-maven: http://maven.aliyun.com/nexus/content/groups/public/
aliyun-ivy: http://maven.aliyun.com/nexus/content/groups/public/, [organization]/[module]/(scala_[scalaVersion]/)(sbt_[sbtVersion]/)[revision]/[type]s/[artifact](-[classifier]).[ext]
typesafe: http://repo.typesafe.com/typesafe/ivy-releases/, [organization]/[module]/(scala_[scalaVersion]/)(sbt_[sbtVersion]/)[revision]/[type]s/[artifact](-[classifier]).[ext], bootOnly
sbt-plugin-ivy: http://dl.bintray.com/sbt/sbt-plugin-releases/, [organization]/[module]/(scala_[scalaVersion]/)(sbt_[sbtVersion]/)[revision]/[type]s/[artifact](-[classifier]).[ext]
dl-ivy: http://dl.bintray.com/typesafe/ivy-releases/, [organization]/[module]/(scala_[scalaVersion]/)(sbt_[sbtVersion]/)[revision]/[type]s/[artifact](-[classifier]).[ext]
sonatype-oss-releases
maven-central

重启sbt

重新打开命令行，切换到\(\mathtt{C: \backslash Users \backslash Shine \backslash .sbt}\)，输入\(\mathtt{sbt \; console}\)命令，更新Scala.
